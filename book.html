Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a photoshoot?
You can book a session by filling out the contact form, calling us, or sending us a WhatsApp message. We'll get back to you within 24 hours.
How long does it take to deliver the photos?
For standard photoshoots, it takes about 1-2 weeks. For wedding albums and cinematic videos, it usually takes 4-6 weeks to ensure the highest quality editing.
Do you travel for destination weddings?
Yes, we do! We love traveling to capture beautiful destination weddings. Travel and accommodation charges apply separately based on the location.
Can we customize the packages?
Absolutely. All our packages can be tailor-made to suit your exact event requirements and budget preferences.
What is the booking process?
Once you fill out the form, we will schedule a consultation to understand your needs. After customizing your package, you pay a deposit to secure your date.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellations made 30 days prior to the event are eligible for a partial refund. The initial booking deposit is non-refundable.
